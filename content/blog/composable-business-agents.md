# Composable Business Agents

_The start of something significant._

AI Agents [are poised](https://darioamodei.com/machines-of-loving-grace#basic-assumptions-and-framework) to surpass human-level intelligence for many tasks.

What does a future look like, where AI agents handle most business processes?

Eventually, business agents will be able to:

1. Access existing information
1. Generate outputs reliably
1. Learn and remember new information
1. Trigger actions safely
1. Publish views
1. Maintain a history of changes

Even though these capabilities are not all available today, the technology is improving rapidly, and there is already value waiting to be unlocked.
